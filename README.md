# Kteb - Book Reading Tracker

A simple Android app to track your reading progress and manage your book library.

## Features

- **Add Books** - Search by book title or enter ISBN to automatically fetch cover images
- **Library** - Organize books by category (Fiction, Comics, Manga, Self-Improvement, Educational, Literature)
- **Reading Timer** - Select a book and track your reading sessions
- **History** - View your reading history and time spent
- **Profile** - Sign in to sync your library
